Republic Day of India

History of Republic Day

Republic Day is celebrated on 26th January every year to honor the day when the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950. It marked the transition of India from a British Dominion to a republic.

Read more about the History of Republic Day

Importance of Republic Day

Republic Day celebrates the adoption of the Constitution of India, which embodies the principles of justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity. It is a day to remember the sacrifices of our freedom fighters.

Read more about the Importance of Republic Day

More Information

Republic Day is celebrated with grandeur across India. The main celebration takes place in New Delhi, where a grand parade is held at Rajpath. The President of India hoists the national flag, and cultural performances showcase India's diversity.
